# Dumble Overdrive Special schematic references

The model targets the Dumble Overdrive Special (ODS), the boutique overdrive/head design best known from the 70s and 90s. The Dumble ODS is built around a multi-stage 12AX7 preamp with a passive mid/treble/bass tone network, followed by a push-pull power stage and presence feedback.

The extracted component/topology map used by the DSP is in `dumble-overdrive-special.toml`. This remains a real-time graybox model, not a SPICE or component-level wave-digital simulation.

The reference includes:

- public schematic scans and notes from Dumble Heaven and EL34World
- ODS builder guides and vintage Dumble service references
- a simplified topology description suitable for a DSP model of the preamp, tone, and power section
